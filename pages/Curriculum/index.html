What about me?

My name is Xavi Coret Mayoral and I am a Computer Engineering student at the Autonomous University of Barcelona(UAB). I love programming, spending hours in front of the computer trying to improve my code and looking for new challenges is what I like to do, that is why I am studying the speciality of Computing of my carreer.

Among my interests and knowledge I can emphasize algorithm and artificial intelligence (AI), the development of web and multi-platform applications and also the game development. I would also like to know more about fields of knowledge such as cybersecurity and, of course, Big Data.

As a computer engineering student, I can say that I am familiar with different programming languages, such as: C,C++ and C#; Python; Java, also for native Android so I also know XML; and those related to web development, that is, HTML, CSS, JQuery, JavaScript and PHP. In these last group I have to say that, for the implementation of dynamic websites as could be an e-commerce, I also know how the Model-View-Controller paradigm works.

I also have knowledge about databases, mainly relational (SQL, SQLite and PostgreSQL) but in some cases I have also worked with non-relational databases such as MongoDB.
