What about me?

My name is Xavi Coret Mayoral and I am a Computer Engineer.
I love programming. Spending hours in front of the computer trying to improve my code and looking for new challenges is what I like to do. This is why I studied Computer Engineering at the Autonomous University of Barcelona(UAB).

My areas of interest and knowledge include algorithms and artificial intelligence (AI), web and multi-platform application development, as well as game development. I would also like to know more about fields of knowledge such as cybersecurity and, of course, Big Data.

As a computer engineering student, I can say that I am familiar with different programming languages, such as: C,C++ and C#; Python; Java, also for native Android so I also know XML; and those related to web development, that is, HTML, CSS, JQuery, JavaScript and PHP. In these last group I have to say that, for the implementation of dynamic websites as could be an e-commerce, I also know how the Model-View-Controller paradigm works.

I also have knowledge about databases, mainly relational (SQL, SQLite and PostgreSQL) but in some cases I have also worked with non-relational databases such as MongoDB.

I learn a lot from my work. One thing that has become a hobby of mine is process automation. I love creating programs that save me time that I would spend doing tasks manually.
In my current job I have developed different automations for file management and folder navigation, as well as tools for digital signature extraction, file comparison and automatic report generation that have allowed to save time and reduce the mistakes made on tasks that we do every day.
