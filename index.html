What about me?

My name is Xavi Coret Mayoral and I am a computer engineer.
I am passionate about programming and love spending hours in front of my computer, constantly improving my code and taking on new challenges. This is why I pursued a degree in Computer Engineering at the Autonomous University of Barcelona(UAB).

My areas of interest and expertise include algorithms, artificial intelligence (AI), web and multi-platform application development, and game development. I am also interested in expanding my knowledge in fields such as cybersecurity and Big Data.

As a computer engineer, I am proficient in various programming languages including C, C++, C#, Python, Java (including Java for Android), as well as web development languages such as HTML, CSS, JQuery, JavaScript and PHP. I am also experienced in implementing dynamic websites, such as e-commerce sites, using the Model-View-Controller paradigm.

In addition to programming, I have experience working with various databases, primarily relational databases such as SQL, SQLite and PostgreSQL, as well as some experience with non-relational databases like MongoDB.

I enjoy learning from my work, and one area of particular interest to me is process automation.
I find it rewarding to create programs that save time, effort, and reduce mistakes by automating repetitive tasks that would otherwise be performed manually. As part of job as Systems Consultant - Source Code Reviewer, I developed various automations for file management and folder navigation, along with tools for digital signature extraction, file comparison, and automatic report generation.
These tools have allowed us to save a significant amount of time and effort on tasks we perform regularly, while also reducing the likelihood of errors. From a business point of view, I believe that these types of process improvements are important in order to increase efficiency and reduce costs.

Portfolio

This section showcases some of the projects that I've worked on throughout my career. These projects have been instrumental in developing my skills and expertise, and I'm excited to share them with you.

Achievements

  • ACE - Associate Cloud Engineer

    In my job as a Frontline TSR for Big Data on Google Cloud Platform at Webhelp, I had the opportunity to become an Associate Cloud Engineer certified by Google Cloud.

Projects

  • TSP - Salesman Problem

    One of the subjects I have studied is Analysis and Design of Algorithms. In this subject, we studied the "Traveling Salesman Problem," designed and applied different algorithms to find the optimal path between two cities, while having to visit other cities in between.

  • IGV Project

    In the "Interactive Graphic Viewing" subject, I had my first contact with 3D objects and OpenGL. I applied knowledge about perspective, lighting, transformation, and animation of objects in both 2D and 3D. I also developed a landscape representation using vectors.

  • Phaser Games

    One of my hobbies is implementing casual games using the Phaser framework for JavaScript. You can see and play some of my games here.

  • Cov-Distance

    Due to the coronavirus outbreak, three colleagues and I participated in a Hackathon on Multimedia Systems. Our app used Google Cloud technologies to measure the distance between pedestrians.

  • Traffic sign recognition

    For my final degree project, I developed an application that could detect, recognize, and provide information about traffic signs in real-time. I implemented convolutional neural networks and computer vision in this project. As a result, computer vision has become one of my favorite fields of study.

  • File signature comparer

    In my job as a Systems Consultant and Source Code Reviewer, I developed a program that is able to compare the files found inside a given path to determine if the files are identical or not.
